If you want your band to be considered for a show at The Crystal Corner, please email us the following information about your band or fill out the form on this page.

  • Facebook link
  • Link to music (Bandcamp, Soundcloud, Spotify)
  • Dates requested
  • Video if you have one
  • When and where you've played in Madison, WI before, how many people showed up, and how much they paid to see you play.
